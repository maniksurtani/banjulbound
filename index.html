What this is about?

Every year a group of idiots decide to drive across Western Africa, from Plymouth in the UK to Banjul in the Gambia in pursuit of fortune and glory, raising money for good causes and to have some adventures along the way. May involve lions. The point is to attempt the drive in a car worth just a few hundred pounds/euros, and if it survives the 3700 mile (5920 km for you EuroTrash readers) journey, it is flogged to the highest bidder with proceeds going to charity. May again involve lions. Yay lions.
